About Gymothy

Gymothy is a workout app that prioritizes user privacy and offers a clean user experience. With no subscriptions and no data tracking, your fitness journey remains personal and secure.

Key Features

  • Unlimited routines and exercises
  • Personalized goals and progress tracking
  • Offline functionality – no internet required
  • Support for "mini-sets" to track breaks within sets
  • Workout history and notes accessible during workouts
  • Apple Watch integration – record workouts without your phone
  • 1800+ exercises with videos and instructions
  • Data sync across devices using Apple's built-in systems
  • Share workouts and routines with friends

Privacy First

Gymothy does not collect any user data. Your workout information stays on your device, ensuring complete privacy.
